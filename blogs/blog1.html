You are living in the matrix.

Recently, the trailer of one of my favorite movie franchises was released- The Matrix Resurrections. For me personally it was a very nostalgic experience. You see, as a child, the matrix franchise was one of those Sci-Fi movies that completely blew my mind. It made a huge impact on the way I perceived the world, so much so, that both my first email id and password had the word ‘MATRIX’ in it. I was completely awestruck by its central premise: ‘you are living in a dream world, Neo’. I, for some reason always thought it to be true, and even today I think there is a bit of truth in it. Here are my reasons why this world is not real and you are dreaming right now:

  • The experience of sleep: There are two kinds of sleep, first is the dreamless, peaceful deep sleep, and the second is the REM (rapid eye movement) sleep, which is the dream state. You may have experienced that while you are dreaming, everything seems real to you. Typically, it doesn’t occur to you that you are dreaming, and you just accept and confirm to whatever reality is being presented to you in the moment.
    What guarantee do you have that you haven’t accepted reality the way it is right now? Also, maybe you are just confirming to the reality as it is right now, without asking the basic questions? You only realize you were dreaming only after you wake up. What if you are dreaming right now and you are going to wake up?

  • Maya: Sanskrit for “Illusion”. The scriptures of several religions have often talked about the human life. When it comes to the Sanathan Dharma (Hinduism), the scriptures mention the concept of Maya. Infact, according to the Hindu mythology, all human beings are merely living in the dreams of Brahma, the God responsible for the creation of the universe.
    On a personal level, Maya is characterized by ‘Ajnana’, i.e., ignorance. Ignorance of the fact that we are all parts of the whole called Brahman- the Supreme Being. It is the dharma of every human being, to realize god, and realize the truth. Have you realized the truth yet? The fact that ancient religious scriptures deal with the concept of Maya strengthens the fact about this world being an illusion.

So how do you convince yourself that you aren’t dreaming right now? Or to put it in a better way, “ Do you think that's air you're breathing now? ”.
